Hiking

Fun Facts

  • Hiking is the fourth most popular outdoor activity in the US
  • The oldest hiking trail in Europe, and possibly the world, is the Via Francigena
  • The Appalachian Trail receives about 2 million visitors annually
  • The longest hiking trail in the world is the aptly named Great Trail which is 14,912 miles

Hiking

Hiking is an activity of moderate difficulty, which involves walking across long distances generally on trails or paths. The duration of the activity varies between short half-day programs and longer itineraries of over 20 days.
